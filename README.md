# Hydrodynamic Simulation of Galaxy Formation

Research project written in LaTeX exploring hydrodynamics simulation of aspects of galaxy formation.

## Contents
- Research paper (PDF)
- LaTeX source code

## Topics
- Fluid dynamics (equation of Euler-Lagrange, Equation of masse concervation, momentum concervation,
- First law of thermodynamics)
- Galaxy formation
- Gravitational collapse
- modèle ΛCDM (Lambda Cold Dark Matter)
-  Equation de Poisson
-  Halo dark matter
-  Supernovae and Black Holes
